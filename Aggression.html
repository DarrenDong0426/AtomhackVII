Micro Aggression

What are Microaggressions? Microaggressions are small things that people say that people tend to look over or even call it “just a joke”, which brings a negative energy towards the group mentioned in the microaggression(intentional or not). Some examples are:

-Calling things “gay” in a deragatory manner

-Assuming that someone is smart because they are Asian

-Normalizing making someone feel uncomfortable but seeing it as a “joke”

-Calling someone autistic or retarted when they actually do not have the disability
